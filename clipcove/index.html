ClipCove Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 23, 2026

ClipCove ("the app") is a clipboard manager and snippet keeper. Everything you save stays on your own device. The app has no accounts, no servers, and does not collect any personal information.

What we collect

Nothing. ClipCove does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data. There is no sign-up, no login, and no user profile.

Where your data lives

Your saved snippets, saved images and app settings are stored locally on your device, in the app's own storage folder. This data never leaves your device and is never sent to us or to any third party.

Backups

Export writes a backup file to a location you choose, and Import reads a backup file you select. These files stay on your device and under your control. The app never uploads them.

Clipboard access

ClipCove reads your clipboard so it can save items you copy, including text, formatted text and images, and writes to your clipboard when you copy a saved snippet. All of this happens entirely on your device. Clipboard contents are never transmitted anywhere.

Payments

The one-time unlock is processed by the platform's app store (for example, Apple's App Store). Payments are handled entirely by the store under its own privacy policy. The app never sees or stores your payment details.

Network use

The app itself does not connect to any server. Links inside the app (such as "Report Issue," "Suggest Feature," and "Support") open in your web browser only when you choose to tap them, taking you to third-party websites that operate under their own privacy policies.

What we don't collect

The app does not collect analytics, telemetry, usage data, or device information. It does not use advertising SDKs.

Third-party distribution

If the app is obtained through the Apple App Store, Microsoft Store, or another platform, the respective platform may process data related to download, purchase, and licensing under their own policies. This policy describes only processing performed by the ClipCove app itself.
