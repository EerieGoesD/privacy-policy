DevDeck Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 24, 2026

DevDeck ("the app") is a desktop app from EERIE for tracking the outstanding work across the projects you maintain. Everything you create stays on your own device. The app has no accounts, no servers, and does not collect any personal information.

What we collect

Nothing. DevDeck does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data. There is no sign-up, no login, and no user profile.

Where your data lives

Your projects, tasks, sub-tasks, notes, dates, templates and app settings are stored locally on your device, in the app's own storage folder, together with two rolling backup copies the app keeps for you. This data never leaves your device and is never sent to us or to any third party. Settings shows you the exact folder and can open it.

Backups

Export writes a backup file to a location you choose, and Import reads a backup file you select. The app can also export a project's task list as a markdown file to a location you choose. These files stay on your device and under your control. The app never uploads them.

Reminders

When you give a task a reminder, the app asks the operating system for permission to show notifications and then shows the reminder on your own device. Nothing about the task is sent anywhere.

Clipboard

When you press the copy button next to a note, the app writes that text to your clipboard. It never reads your clipboard, and nothing is sent anywhere.

Network use

The app works fully offline and does not connect to any server. Its fonts are bundled inside the app, so no web fonts are fetched. The only time a connection is opened is when you choose to click a link, such as the footer links or a link you wrote in a task's notes, which opens that page in your default browser under that site's own privacy policy.

What we don't collect

The app does not collect analytics, telemetry, usage data, or device information. It does not use advertising SDKs.

Third-party distribution

If the app is obtained through the Microsoft Store, Apple App Store, or another platform, the respective platform may process data related to download, purchase, and licensing under their own policies. This policy describes only processing performed by the DevDeck app itself.
